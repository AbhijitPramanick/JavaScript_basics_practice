String methods in JS

Length method

1. Length of "ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ" : text.length

2. Slice of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.slice(7, 13)

3. Slice of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.slice(7)

4. Slice of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.slice(-12)

5. Slice of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.slice(-12,-6)

Substring method

6. Substring of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substring(7, 13)

7. Substring of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substring(7)

8. Substring of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substring(-12)

9. Substring of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substring(-12,-6)

Substr method

10. substr of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substr(7, 13)

11. substr of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substr(7)

12. substr of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substr(-12)

13. substr of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substr(-12,-6)

✔ The two parameters of substr() are start and length , while for substring() , they are start and end . substr() 's start index will wrap to the end of the string if it is negative, while substring() will clamp it to 0 .

14. substr of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substr(-12)

15. substr of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substr(-12,6)

✔ Negative lengths in substr() are treated as zero, while substring() will swap the two indexes if end is less than start.

16. substr of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substr(12,-3)

17. Substring of "Apple, Banana, Kiwi" : text.substring(-12,6)
